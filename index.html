Serious Game Prototype

Crisis Mode

You are the founder of a fast-growing startup facing cascading crises. Balance Reputation, Cash, and Team Morale through five pressure rounds.

  • Choose one action each round. Every choice has trade-offs.
  • Some choices create delayed consequences in later rounds.
  • If any core metric hits 0, your startup collapses immediately.
  • Survive all 5 rounds to win and receive your leadership profile.
